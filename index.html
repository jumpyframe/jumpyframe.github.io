Universal skeletal animation tool

What is JumpyFrame?

It's a tool that focus on skeletal character animation. It enables a simple way to build skeletons from scratch and author animation clips for multiple purpose. Bring characters to life using touch gestures and deploy to your game engine or animation software. 
It's not about an animation tool it's about knowing how to animate!
